Our Story

About FreeLegalFormat.in

We believe every Indian citizen — regardless of education or income — deserves access to simple, professional legal documents. So we built the tools to make it happen.

🎯

Our Mission

To make legal documents simple, accessible, and completely free for every Indian citizen. We want to remove the barriers of language, cost, and complexity that prevent ordinary people from asserting their rights.

Whether you're filing a police complaint, demanding a refund, or creating a GST invoice — you should be able to do it yourself, without paying a lawyer for a basic format.

🔭

Our Vision

A future where every Indian — from a farmer in rural Bihar to a shopkeeper in Mumbai — can create, understand, and use professional legal documents in their own language, with zero cost and zero complexity.

We envision FreeLegalFormat.in becoming India's most trusted free legal document platform, covering every type of document a common citizen might need.

How It Started

Our Story

💡

The Problem We Saw

A relative needed to file a police complaint after being cheated online. They visited a local lawyer who charged ₹500 just to write a basic complaint letter — something that should take 5 minutes using a standard format.

We searched online and found only confusing, outdated PDF formats scattered across random government websites. Nothing was easy, nothing was in simple Hindi, and nothing generated the draft automatically.

🔨

What We Built

We built FreeLegalFormat.in — a simple, browser-based tool that generates professional legal documents in seconds. No login, no payment, no complicated forms. Just fill in your details and download.

We made it available in English, Hindi, and Bengali to make sure language is never a barrier. And because everything runs in your browser, your data is 100% private — we never see what you type.

🚀

Where We're Going

We are constantly adding new document types, more languages, and smarter tools. Our roadmap includes RTI applications, rent agreements, legal notices, and consumer court complaint formats. All free. Always.
